Greetings! I am Dmitry.
A few words about me.

Education.
Born in Ukraine in the city of Kharkov. He graduated from high school in the same city. He studied at the National Aerospace University (KhAI) with a degree in: "Radio engineering devices, systems and complexes." In 2002 he defended his diploma for a Bachelor's degree, and in 2003 for a Specialist's degree (Theme of work: "Development of voice signal compression algorithms in DECT and GSM900/1800 mobile communication systems"). In 2006 he graduated from the courses of managers.

Profession.
For the last twenty years, my main activity has been everything related to engineering. He went all the way from an employee to the chief engineer of the enterprise. This path took 12 years in peacetime. Worked both in the staff of companies and as a freelancer. The main direction is automated process control systems in food production and public catering establishments. Performed a full cycle of work on the development, creation and maintenance of power supply, ventilation, air conditioning and refrigeration systems for facilities. A full stack of work: customer search, statement of technical specifications, selection of equipment, development of an electrical circuit and documentation, assembly and installation (installation supervision) of equipment, warranty and service maintenance, modernization of outdated systems. As a freelance consultant for engineering communications, in addition to the main ones, he solved issues of water supply, sanitation and other related tasks.

Hobby.
I like to learn and apply new technologies in practice in my work. In addition, I like to create something new, something that is not there yet. Therefore, all my hobbies in one way or another arose out of ordinary curiosity. For example, flocking, phosphors, heat accumulator. I study technologies in detail and mix (combine) them. As a result, a unique product or a new direction for development appears. For example, the results of the hobby were: collections of leather goods of our own design, using modern technologies in 2011 and 2020; from idea to packaging, a decor series using phosphors in 2015; a patent for a utility model of a heat accumulator and a working prototype in 2021.

Computer programs.
